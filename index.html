Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Amélia?

Graduated from University Paris 8 with a degree in Digital Creation & Editing, Amélia is passionate about digital design, reflected in every project she has had the opportunity to develop, from initial ideation to the creation of the final prototype.

What is Amélia's experience in the field of design?

Amélia has three years of experience in web and UI design. Her experience spans a wide spectrum of activities in the design field, including user research, creating interactive prototypes, web design, and user testing.

What tools and skills does Amélia possess for design and development?

Amélia is highly proficient with the Adobe Creative Suite, using it for various graphic and video design projects. She is a master at Figma, utilizing it extensively for UI design. Furthermore, Amélia is skilled in web development, capable of creating websites using JavaScript and enhancing them with Three.js for immersive 3D experiences. Additionally, she is experienced in responsive design, ensuring optimal user experience across devices, and has a solid understanding of user-centered design principles to create engaging and intuitive interfaces.

What is Amélia's professional goal?

Amélia's goal is to transform innovative ideas into captivating and functional digital experiences.
